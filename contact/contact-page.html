About:


$\text{S}\small{\LaTeX}$ is an interactive $\TeX$ editor built off of C++ and the SDL2 library. It's designed as a user friendly application for creating presentations and documents with $\TeX$. The application is free and open source and constantly being developed. Feel free to contribute to the project on its Github.
